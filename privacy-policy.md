# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

COCO ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What We Collect

- **Account information**: email address and password (stored securely via Supabase)
- **Profile information**: display name, bio, hometown, profile photo
- **Climbing data**: sessions, climbs, grades, notes, locations you enter
- **Usage data**: app activity needed to sync your data across devices

## What We Don't Collect

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not collect data for advertising purposes
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and sync your climbing log across devices
- To power the friends and social features you opt into
- To allow you to delete your account and all associated data at any time

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. All data is protected by Row Level Security — you can only access your own data.

## Your Rights

- You can edit or delete your profile at any time from the app
- You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
- You can contact us to request a copy of your data
